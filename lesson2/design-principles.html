Design Principles Document

Darrin Fry

Alignment

Disney Parks Vacations

We see that all the objects and images are nicely aligned inside of their elements. The padding on each one gives a clean and organized feel. The alignment provides a nested feeling that helps to compartmentalize the website.

Fitt's Law

The Chateaux at Deer Valley

The button on the bottom right is easy to get to, stays in place, and takes you to their call to action: namely the bookings. Additionally, the bar on the bottom does the same thing, making the ease of access even greater.

White Space

Seven Oaks Fine Jewelers

The white space is repeated throughout the website highlighting the images, buttons, and text. This improves the usability of the site. Also, the white space encourages simplicity in design and aids the visual hierarchy.
