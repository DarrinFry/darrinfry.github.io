The Lazy River Adventure

For those a little more relaxed, try the Lazy River Adventure. Enjoy the scenery, listen to the wildlife, maybe even catch a fish or two. This scenic ride is perfect for anyone looking to enjoy the river.

  • Skill Level:

    Novice through expert

  • Length:

    Single day, two days, and three days

  • Services:

    Instruction, food, travel to and from the river, and overnight accommodations (camping) for two and three-day trips.

  • Cost:

    Starting at $195 per person. *Cost varies based on season, length of trip, and size of group. Please call for deatails.

The Kayaking Adventure

The Kayaking Adventure combines the thrill of small rapids with a slow and scenic pace. Enjoy a little distance as you and your group take control of your own individual vessel.

  • Skill Level:

    Intermediate through expert

  • Length:

    Single day and two days

  • Services:

    Instruction, food, travel to and from the river, and overnight accommodations (camping) for two day trips.

  • Cost:

    Starting at $235 per person. *Cost varies based on season, length of trip, and size of group. Please call for deatails.

The "I hope grandpa doesn't fall out" Adventrue

Need a little more adrenaline in your life? This trip includes a few calm patches in between blood pumping rapids. This trip is not for the faint at heart, so please consult your physician to confirm that you are healthy enough to participate.

  • Skill Level:

    Advanced through expert

  • Length:

    Single day and two days and three days

  • Services:

    Instruction, food, travel to and from the river, and overnight accommodations (camping) for two and three-day trips.

  • Cost:

    Starting at $295 per person. *Cost varies based on season, length of trip, and size of group. Please call for deatails.
