5 Day Forcast

Winter is almost over!!

As spring approaches and winter leaves us, we find our beautiful reservoirs filling with plenty of fresh, clean water. Due to the recent increase in growth in the Preston area, we have been asked if we will have enough water. Luckily, yes, we will have plenty of water to go around. We want to remind you of the importance of using this precious water wisely so that we can continue to have an ample supply. First, water your yards deeply and on alternating schedules. Second, showers use less water than a bath, so consider showering with a friend (it is more fun!) Third, don't leave your faucets running while you brush your teeth, this wastes water. This is also the part of the paragraph where I randomly say things to see if anyone actually reads these things; if so, I hope you are having a great day! Remember to wash your hands and don't touch your face.
