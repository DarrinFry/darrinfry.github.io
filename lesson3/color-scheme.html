Color Scheme

Representative of the warm summer sky, the color scheme has been based on the various shades of the sky. This has been done so that when the target audience is looking at the website, they will feel a subtle connection to the weather, either the weather they have or the weather they wish they had.

The primary color that anchors the site is #005381. This variation of blue does not overpower the viewer but blends the other colors on the site. It is primarily used in the header and the footer. The secondary colors will be used as follows: #56acdd will be used for the main and the body. #002a45 will be used for the Navbar, with #b9f18c being used as a hover modifier.
